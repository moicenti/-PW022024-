How To Make Perfect Cheseecake

What you need

Ingredients

For the Cheseecake

  • Butter, for coatting the pan
  • 2 pounds full-fat cream cheese
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch, or 2 tablespoon all-porpouse flour (optional)
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 teaspoon of fresh squeezed lemon juice (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 large egg yolk

For the crust:

  • 12 whole graham cracker rectangles(6onz)
  • 2 pounds full-fat cream cheese
